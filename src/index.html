The Women Behind Alem Spa

Alem’s Story — Healing Through Culture, Care, and Tradition

Alem created this spa to share the traditional healing rituals she grew up with in Ethiopia — practices rooted in warmth, purity, and feminine renewal. Her journey began long before AlemSpa existed, shaped by family traditions, cultural wisdom, and a deep desire to help women feel soft, cleansed, and reconnected with their inner peace.

With a gentle presence and a devotion to natural wellness, Alem brings authenticity to every treatment, from the Ethiopian Yoni Smoke ceremony to the Moroccan Bath experience. Every woman who steps into her care is welcomed with respect, tenderness, and the intention to restore balance to both body and spirit.
